Robostorm: Team 12601 About Us

We are Robostorm FTC Team 12601 from Hunterdon County new Jersey. We are a rookie first year team team. We are a 4H club witch menas that we are open to all kids in Hunterdon County grades 7-12. Our entire robot was custom machined in one of our members home machine shop. This year we attended New Jersey states and advanced to Eastern Super Regionals.

About First Tech Challenge

The FIRST Tech Challenge is a robotics competition that allows students to compete head to head, and are chalenged to design, build, program, and operate robots.
